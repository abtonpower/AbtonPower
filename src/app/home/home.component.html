Our Journey

For over 25 years, Abton Power Devices Pvt Ltd has been a leading provider of top-tier power backup solutions in Delhi, South Delhi, and the NCR region. We offer a comprehensive range of products to ensure your home, business, or industrial needs are met during power outages. As a team of experienced electrical engineers, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and quality solutions to meet our customers' needs. Our passion for innovation and attention to detail have earned us a reputation as a leading provider of power backup solutions. We work tirelessly to stay within budget while providing maximum value for our clients.
